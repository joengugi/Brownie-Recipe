A RECIPE TO PREPARE BROWNIES

To prepare yummy brownies, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • An egg
  • 1 cake mix
  • 1/2 cup white chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup extra sprinkles

The procedure is as follows:

  1. Preheat your oven to 360 degrees
  2. Mix together the cake mix,milk, egg and melted butter in a bowl
  3. Ensure the mixture is thick and smooth with no lumps
  4. Add extra sprinkles and the white chocolate chips then stir to distribute
  5. Pour the mixture in a baking dish and bake for 15-20 minutes
  6. You'll know the brownies are ready when a toothpick can be inserted in them and come out clean

I hope you'll enjoy the brownies!!
